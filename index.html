This collection of slides form the course materials for the Web and Mobile Development course, part of the Professional Bachelor ICT study programme, taught at KaHo Sint-Lieven, Ghent, Belgium.

The materials were developed by Bram(us) Van Damme, lecturer ICT at KaHo Sint-Lieven, who blogs over at bram.us and Twitters as @bramus.

The materials may be used freely, as long as credit to Bramus is present and the top right graphical link to ikdoeict.be remains in place.
